Interactive accelerometer explainer showing X, Y, Z values changing as you tilt a virtual micro:bit

The accelerometer measures how strongly gravity (and movement) pulls in three directions. Flat on a desk, only gravity pulls — that's the Z axis. When you tilt, X and Y change. Try the sliders or tap an example.
