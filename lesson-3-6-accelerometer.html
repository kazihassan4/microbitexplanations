Accelerometer — what do the numbers mean?

The accelerometer measures force in three directions at once

When your micro:bit is flat on a desk, only gravity pulls — that shows up on the Z axis as roughly −1024. When you tilt it, X and Y change. All three numbers update continuously. Use the sliders or tap a preset below to explore.
